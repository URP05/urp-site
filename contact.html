CONTACT • CONNECT WITH URP

Contact us directly

For suggestions, complaints, local issues, research inputs or collaboration — you can reach URP directly. Your feedback and experience will be an important input for building better policies in the future.

Central contact (state level)

Email: contact@uttarakhandreform.org
Phone: +91 98765 43210
Timings: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Monday–Saturday)

Your message will be routed to the relevant district or team. Please mention a clear subject such as “Suggestion”, “Complaint”, “Local issue” or “Join as volunteer”.

District coordination

Each district will have a district coordinator responsible for membership, volunteers and local meetings.

For now, please mention your district and assembly constituency clearly in your email. A team member will contact you by phone or WhatsApp at a convenient time.

Social media & updates

Official social media pages and channels will be launched in phases. As they become active, details will be shared on this page.

The aim is to regularly share policy information, short videos, ground reports from the hills and important announcements in a transparent manner.

Want to send a local issue or ground report?

If you wish to describe a local issue in detail — related to roads, water, schools, hospitals, forests, migration, employment or any other topic — please:

  • Mention district and assembly seat in the email subject line.
  • Explain the problem, its impact on people and what you think could be a practical solution.
  • Attach photos or relevant documents if available.

Such information will be used as input while designing policies and future public commitments.
