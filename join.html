JOIN • MEMBERSHIP & VOLUNTEERS

Join URP — contribute to constructive politics

Membership in Uttarakhand Reform Party is simple, digital and transparent. There are no hidden forms or closed groups — aware citizens, ex-servicemen, youth, women and professionals are all welcome.

Four simple steps to become a member

  • Fill the online form: Complete the Google Form below with your name, mobile number, EPIC, district and assembly details.
  • Verification: A district / assembly coordinator will contact you within 7–10 days through call or WhatsApp.
  • Decide your role: Together we will decide how you would like to contribute — as a volunteer, at booth/ward level or in digital teams.
  • Join an official team: You will be added to official WhatsApp / email groups and informed about meetings and trainings.
No joining fee at present

Who can join?

  • Anyone 18 years or above with a strong connection to Uttarakhand (resident, voter or origin).
  • People from all communities, faiths, regions and languages — with zero discrimination.
  • Persons not convicted in any serious criminal case.
