ABOUT URP

Who We Are

Uttarakhand Reform Party (URP) is a citizen-led political platform focused on strengthening governance, improving public accountability and creating balanced development across Uttarakhand. Our approach is rooted in practicality, transparency and long-term structural reform.

Our Background

URP emerged from continuous interaction with citizens, professionals, social workers and community leaders who shared a common concern — the need for visible improvement in governance quality and delivery systems. The party focuses on structured planning, local engagement and measurable development outcomes rather than personality-driven politics.

  • Community-driven political formation.
  • Focus on accountability and institutional reform.
  • Inclusive representation from diverse regions of Uttarakhand.

Core Philosophy

URP believes political leadership must be guided by ethical governance, social responsibility and sustainable development models that strengthen both urban and rural communities.

Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a transparent political environment that respects public trust, encourages citizen participation and focuses on practical problem-solving.

Core Values

  • Integrity and Transparency in decision-making.
  • Equal respect for all regions and communities.
  • Youth and women participation in leadership roles.
  • Evidence-based governance and policy planning.

Organisational Culture

  • Inclusive internal structure and open dialogue.
  • Ground-level participation and decentralised leadership.
  • Transparency in operations and communication.
  • Continuous learning and accountability mechanisms.
